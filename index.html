STAR BATTLE

a game by microwerx for LDJAM 45 "Start with Nothing"
Controls
Description & Notes

STAR BATTLE is a game where you build and mine your own star system. Collect Creation Stars to spawn a star in EXO mode. Then explore your system in ENDO mode. Try different sizes of galaxies by adjusting the level. How much gold can you get?

Use WASD controls to move.

SPACE bar controls ENDO/EXO mode.

ENTER places a star in EXO mode.
